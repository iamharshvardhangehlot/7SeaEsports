ABOUT 7SEA

One of the fastest-growing esports organizations in India

7Sea Esports, is on a mission to give young Indians a platform to showcase their talents to a wider audience. Over ten of 7Sea's active pros compete at the highest levels of the country's most popular games.

While 7SEA's current roster of brand ambassadors includes several well-known names from the world of Indian gaming and esports, the organization has big hopes to expand its network of young-targeted industries like Indian hip-hop and streetwear.
